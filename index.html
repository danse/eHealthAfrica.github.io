eHealth Africa Ebola Response

We are eHealth Africa, an NGO based in northern Nigeria doing innovative health service delivery and outbreak response projects in West Africa. We're looking for talented, hard-working javascript engineers to join our Berlin dev team immediately and work on our live ebola outbreak response systems.

You must
  • Have excellent JavaScript skills
  • Have professional experience with AngularJS and/or document storage (preferably CouchDB)
  • Have a sense of humor under pressure
  • Seriously love good coding / design practices
  • Be familiar with basic devops, git, TDD, git, RESTful APIs
It would be extra awesome if you
  • Were into data analysis & visualization, GIS, node, django, and/or cordova/phonegap apps
  • Considered yourself a full-stack dev with side interests in UX design
You'll get
  • To work with a dynamic international team
  • Using cutting edge web technologies
  • Open-sourcing most of your code
  • From Berlin (with the possibility to visit Nigeria)
  • On a critical, innovative project with international significance

We're after folks who can begin immediately, initial contract will be 3 months but could be extended. If you're interested in chatting about it please send a short note describing your best work and a resume to Justin at ebola.dev@ehealthnigeria.org.
